# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

## Introduction

WeSearchAI ("we," "us," or "our") operates the WeSearchAI website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our site.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Comments and Feedback**: When you leave comments or contact us
- **Email Subscriptions**: When you subscribe to our RSS feed or newsletter
- **Contact Forms**: Any information submitted through contact forms

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages
- **Cookies**: Small files stored on your device for functionality and analytics
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique identifiers

### Third-Party Information

- **Google Analytics**: Traffic and behavior analytics
- **Google AdSense**: Advertising-related data
- **Social Media**: If you interact through social platforms

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- Site improvement and optimization
- Personalizing user experience
- Analyzing site traffic and performance
- Sending newsletters (if subscribed)
- Responding to inquiries
- Complying with legal obligations

## Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:
- Website functionality
- User preferences
- Analytics and traffic monitoring
- Advertising personalization

You can control cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit site functionality.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics
Google collects data about site usage. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Google AdSense
AdSense uses cookies for interest-based advertising. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Third-Party Links
Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for external sites' privacy practices.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your Rights
